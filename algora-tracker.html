No. It's a plain markdown file. Works in Obsidian, VS Code, iA Writer, or any text editor. Notion-compatible structure is included as a bonus.

Algora-specific by design, since that's where the highest-quality bounties are. But the structure works for any GitHub bounty platform (GitHub Bounties, LayerCI, etc.) with minor field tweaks.
